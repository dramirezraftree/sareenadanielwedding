The Basics
When and where is the wedding?

The wedding weekend takes place on Friday, November 27th and Saturday, November 28th, 2026 at The Windflower Resort & Spa in Varca, South Goa, India. Checkout is Sunday, November 29th.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Plus-ones will be specified in your invitation. If you have any questions about your invitation, feel free to reach out to us directly.

Do you have a registry?

Yes! You can find our registry on the Registry page — it'll take you straight to our Zola. Thank you so much for thinking of us.

Do I need a visa to visit India?

Yes, most international visitors will need a visa to enter India. The good news is that India's e-Visa is straightforward to apply for online and is available to citizens of most countries. We recommend applying at least a month before your travel date — check the application window requirements for your nationality and don't leave it too late. Check indianvisaonline.gov.in for the latest requirements based on your nationality.

Travel & Getting There
Which airport should I fly into?

It depends on when you're arriving. If you're coming directly for the wedding weekend, fly into Dabolim Airport (GOI) in South Goa — it's about 25–35 minutes from the venue. If you're joining us early for the pre-wedding days in North Goa (November 24–26), fly into Mopa Airport (GOX) instead.
